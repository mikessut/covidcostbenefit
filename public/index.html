COVID-19 Cost Benefit

In an effort to encourage thought about the consequences of policies and their tradeoffs, this site strives to crowd source what some of the trade offs of policy decisions are related to the COVID-19 pandemic. The site is very much a work in progress at the moment and has been an opportunity for me to learn some web development tools. It is open source, and if you have any suggestions, please don't hesitate to contact me and submit a pull request from the repo.
